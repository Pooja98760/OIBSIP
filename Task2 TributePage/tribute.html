Abdul Kalam

1931-2015

Biography

October 15, 1931, Rameswaram, India—died July 27,
2015, Shillong), Indian scientist and politician who played
a leading role in the development of India’s missile
and nuclear weapons programs.
He was president of India from 2002 to 2007.
Kalam earned a degree in aeronautical engineering
from the Madras Institute of Technology and in 1958
joined the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO).
He is also called as Missile Man of India.
